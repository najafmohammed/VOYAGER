Brief Overview

Voyager is an Role Playing Game where the player manages a spaceship and travels across the galaxy in search of a habitable planet for humanity. Due to negligence of humans , earth is close to being inhabitable and was forced move to Mars and others space outposts situated around ther moons of planets.But the sun in dying and there humans are on verge of extinction On one of those moons humans discover a ship and hope for the survival of humanity.

Plot

Due to human activities earth has become nearly inhabitable forcing people to move to other outposts and moons to survive but the sun is dying and they need to move to other habitable planets to survive. But technology has not progressed so far as to allow humans to travel beyond the solar system. This is when a ship is discovered by miners that went to mine an asteroid that was comming from outside the solar system. Due to the dire situation governmeents around the world has united into a singular nation . The ship on inspection seems to be designed by humans as it closely resembled the ships that they currently had. This proved that there are habitable planets out there with inteligent lifeforms Inside the ship there seems to be an AI that understands humans and says that it was send here to save humanity. The Ship can create wormholes between two places which will allow people to travel great distance quickly. The player is the top pilot of humanity and is assinged a crew to pilot the ship to new planets.

The player will face a lot of adventure in the game exploring the galaxy and saving humanity.
